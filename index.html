About me

I graduated with an MSc in Mathematical Sciences (Major: Fundamental Sciences) with distinction from the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS) Cameroon, affiliated with the University of Buea, Cameroon, on June 23rd, 2023. My research work was entitled "Carmichael Numbers." Prior to AIMS, I obtained a Bachelor's Degree (with mention) in fundamental mathematics from the Faculty of Science, University of Fianarantsoa, Madagascar. Additionally, I have completed all the required courses for my master's degree and am currently writing my research master thesis, which focuses on the field of artificial intelligence.

Through my studies, I gained valuable experience in team working, communication skills, and project management, especially during my stay at AIMS. Additionally, I strengthened my mathematical background, knowledge of computer programming, problem-solving, and research skills. My research interests include number theory, cryptography, machine learning, and deep learning.

Besides my academic pursuits, I am a self-taught Python programmer, with experience in web programming (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc.) and database management, such as MySql. I have worked on personal projects, and I continuously learn new thing, and improve what I already know.
